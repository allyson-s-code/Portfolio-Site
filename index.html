A little bit about me.

I’m an artist living in Brooklyn, NY with a newfound love of coding. My journey into tech started with a degree in fine art and experience in luxury home product design. I found that I was equally at home with the creative process and the analytic process of translating designs into specs.

When I started coding websites I realized I found something that combined my love of both aspects. Now I have a career where I can use my attention to detail and creative problem solving skills everyday. I’m loving the discoveries along the way!

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.
